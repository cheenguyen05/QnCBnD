Who's there? The BOYNEXTDOOR!

BOYNEXTDOOR (보이넥스트도어) (can be shorten to BND) is a 6-member South Korean boy group under KOZ Entertainment, consisting of Jaehyun, Sungho, Riwoo, Taesan, Leehan, and Woonhak. They debuted on May 30, 2023, with their first single album, WHO!. They made their Japanese debut on July 10, 2024.

Group Name Meaning: They’re the boys who live next door. They’ll be singing songs that a lot of people can hopefully relate to in their daily lives, as friendly as their name. Official Greeting: “Who’s there? BOYNEXTDOOR! Hello∼, we’re BOYNEXTDOOR!”

BOYNEXTDOOR Official Fandom Name: ONEDOOR (원도어) Fandom Name Meaning: The fans of BOYNEXTDOOR are the only ONEDOOR that can connect BOYNEXTDOOR to the world.
